Hunza Valley

We, PakView Travels, are excited to offer you an unforgettable experience in the mesmerizing Hunza Valley, a hidden gem nestled in the heart of northern Pakistan. Our specially curated 7-day tour will allow you to explore the breathtaking landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and serene beauty of this enchanting destination. During this trip, you’ll be staying at the luxurious Hunza Serena Hotel, known for its exceptional service, stunning views, and modern amenities. The hotel is perfectly situated to offer you the best of Hunza, with rooms overlooking the majestic mountains and lush greenery. Your adventure begins with a scenic drive through the captivating landscapes of Gilgit-Baltistan, passing lush valleys, terraced fields, and rugged mountains. On Day 1, we will arrive in Hunza and check into the Hunza Serena Hotel. After settling in, you'll have some time to relax and enjoy the stunning panoramic views from your room. The next few days will be filled with sightseeing and exploration. You'll visit Baltit Fort, a 700-year-old historical site, and Altit Fort, both showcasing the rich history of the region. Take a stroll around the charming streets of Karimabad and explore the traditional Hunza culture. On Day 3, you’ll visit the mesmerizing Passu Cones and Khunjerab Pass, the highest border crossing between Pakistan and China, offering panoramic views that will leave you in awe. Additionally, you'll enjoy a peaceful visit to Attabad Lake, where you can take a boat ride on the crystal-clear waters surrounded by towering mountains. We also include a day trip to Husseini Suspension Bridge, one of the most dangerous bridges in the world, for the adventurous at heart. Throughout the trip, we will provide a comfortable and safe travel experience, ensuring you make the most of your time in Hunza. Our experienced guides will provide valuable insights into the history, culture, and natural wonders of Hunza Valley, making this an enriching experience. We also take care of all meals during the trip, with traditional Hunza cuisine served at local restaurants, allowing you to taste the authentic flavors of the region. Whether you’re an adventure enthusiast or someone seeking peace and tranquility, this trip will cater to all your needs. Book your spot now with PakView Travels and embark on a once-in-a-lifetime journey to Hunza Valley, where nature and culture blend seamlessly to create an unforgettable experience!
