Azad Kashmir

Your trip to Azad Kashmir will be an unforgettable and peaceful experience, filled with stunning natural beauty, historical sites, and a chance to immerse yourself in the rich local culture. Our tour package includes everything from transportation to accommodation, meals, and guided tours, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable journey from start to finish. The journey will begin from Islamabad, where you'll be picked up in a comfortable vehicle and driven to Azad Kashmir. Our experienced guides will provide you with in-depth information about each location, making sure you have a safe and informative trip. During the tour, you will visit some of the most popular and scenic destinations, including Muzaffarabad, Neelum Valley, Rawalakot, and Ratti Gali Lake. Accommodation will be provided in comfortable hotels or guesthouses, where you’ll enjoy modern amenities such as air-conditioned rooms, Wi-Fi, and delicious meals. Your days will be filled with sightseeing and exploring these beautiful destinations, and in the evenings, you can relax and unwind in your room, enjoying a peaceful atmosphere. Each day, you'll be treated to local cuisine, which is an essential part of the experience and adds to the overall charm of your trip. During this tour, you'll have the opportunity to visit many iconic spots, such as the tranquil valleys of Neelum Valley, the hilltops of Pir Chinasi, and the natural beauty of Rawalakot. You’ll also get a chance to visit Ratti Gali Lake, one of the most scenic lakes in Pakistan. You'll be able to take stunning photographs and create lasting memories of your time in Azad Kashmir. Our all-inclusive tour package covers transportation, accommodation, meals, and guided tours. All you need to do is relax and enjoy the incredible beauty of Azad Kashmir. The cost of the package depends on the duration of your trip and the size of your group, but we guarantee that it will be a budget-friendly and value-for-money experience. Our company is committed to providing you with a memorable and enjoyable trip, allowing you to share these precious moments with friends and family.
